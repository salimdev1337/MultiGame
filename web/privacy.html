Privacy Policy

MultiGame  ·  Last updated: February 24, 2026

1. What we collect

MultiGame collects the minimum data required to provide the service:

  • Account information – email address and display name when you sign up via Firebase Authentication.
  • Game statistics – scores, move counts, and achievement data stored in Cloud Firestore so your progress syncs across devices.
  • Device information – anonymous crash reports via Firebase Crashlytics (device model, OS version, app version). No personal identifiers are included.

2. What we do not collect

  • We do not sell, share, or rent your personal data to third parties.
  • We do not collect precise location data.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not serve third-party advertising.

3. Local WiFi multiplayer

When you host or join a local WiFi game (Bomberman, Wordle Duel, Rummy), your device's local IP address is used to establish a direct peer connection on your network. This address is never sent to our servers and is discarded when the game session ends.

4. Image content (Unsplash)

The Image Puzzle game loads photos from the Unsplash API. Unsplash's own privacy policy applies to those requests. We do not store or forward your Unsplash requests.

5. Data retention

Your account and game data are retained for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion at any time by contacting us. We will remove your data within 30 days.

6. Children

MultiGame is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Security

Sensitive data (tokens, session keys) is stored using device-level secure storage (flutter_secure_storage). Data in transit is encrypted via TLS. Firebase services are configured with strict Firestore security rules that prevent users from reading or modifying other users' data.
