## 1. Introduction

Welcome to MultiGame. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information Automatically Collected

When you use MultiGame, we automatically collect certain information about your device and usage:

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: Game sessions, scores, completion times, game preferences
- **Technical Data**: App version, crash reports, performance metrics

### 2.2 Information You Provide

- **Nickname**: Optional display name for leaderboards (stored locally on your device)
- **Game Statistics**: Scores, achievements, puzzle completions
- **Settings Preferences**: Sound, haptic feedback, difficulty preferences

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do NOT collect personally identifiable information (name, email, phone number, address)
- We do NOT collect location data
- We do NOT collect photos or camera access
- We do NOT collect contacts or calendar data
- We do NOT use cookies or tracking pixels

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

- **Game Functionality**: Track scores, achievements, and progress
- **Leaderboards**: Display anonymous rankings based on game performance
- **App Improvement**: Analyze usage patterns to improve gameplay and fix bugs
- **Performance Monitoring**: Identify and resolve technical issues
- **User Experience**: Save your settings and preferences

## 4. Third-Party Services

MultiGame uses the following third-party services:

### 4.1 Firebase (Google)

- **Purpose**: Backend infrastructure for authentication, database, and analytics
- **Data Collected**: Anonymous user ID, game scores, achievements, session data
- **Privacy Policy**: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
- **Authentication**: We use Firebase Anonymous Authentication. No email, password, or personal information is required.

### 4.2 Unsplash API (Optional)

- **Purpose**: Fetch random images for puzzle games
- **Data Collected**: None directly; Unsplash may log API requests per their policy
- **Privacy Policy**: https://unsplash.com/privacy
- **Note**: This feature falls back to local images if API is unavailable

## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Local Storage

- Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage
- Nicknames and preferences are never transmitted to our servers
- You can clear local data at any time through your device settings

### 5.2 Cloud Storage (Firebase Firestore)

- Game scores and achievements are stored in Firebase Firestore
- Each user is assigned an anonymous, randomly-generated ID
- Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
- We implement security rules to prevent unauthorized access

### 5.3 Data Retention

- Game statistics are retained indefinitely to maintain leaderboard integrity
- Inactive accounts (no activity for 3+ years) may be anonymized or deleted
- Local data persists until you uninstall the app or clear app data

## 6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, rent, or share your data with third parties, except:

- **Service Providers**: Firebase/Google for app infrastructure (subject to their privacy policies)
- **Legal Requirements**: If required by law, court order, or government regulation
- **Safety**: To protect our rights, property, safety, or the safety of others

## 7. Children's Privacy
